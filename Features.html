Real-Time Fleet Tracking

  • Monitor the exact location of every bus in your fleet with live updates.
  • Keep track of each bus's speed to ensure compliance with safety standards.
  • Receive immediate notifications for over-speeding and other critical events.
  • Ensure passenger and operational safety with timely insights.
  • Optimize fleet performance through comprehensive live monitoring and alert systems.

Driver App - Empowering Drivers

  • Enable drivers to manage passenger drop-ins and drop-offs seamlessly using QR codes.
  • Drivers can instantly notify admins in case of emergencies for swift and effective action.
  • Drivers can enable their location for real-time tracking and accurate route guidance.

User App - Keeping Passengers Informed

  • Passengers can track the bus in real-time, knowing exactly when it will arrive.
  • Get accurate estimated times of arrival for pickups and drop-offs.
  • Stay informed about any delays, route changes, or other important events during the trip..
  • Ensure seamless communication between passengers, guardians, and drivers for a smooth experience..

Advanced Fleet Monitoring & Insights

  • Track bus locations, speed, and status in real-time from a centralized dashboard.
  • Receive immediate notifications for over-speeding, route deviations, and other significant incidents.
  • Access historical data, monitor performance trends, and generate reports to enhance fleet efficiency..
  • Easily monitor attendance, generate detailed reports, and ensure operational transparency..

Instant Emergency Response

  • Attendants can quickly trigger SOS alerts during emergencies, ensuring immediate attention and support.
  • Instantly connect with the attendant through a live conference call for direct communication and swift resolution.
  • Admins can track the exact location of the emergency and identify nearby vehicles ready to assist.
  • All communication records and media shared by the attendant are easily accessible and downloadable from the admin portal for further review.
