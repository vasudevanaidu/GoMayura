How GoMayura Works!!

Say goodbye to concerns about passenger safety while traveling to and from their destination. Live tracking and timely notifications keep you informed every step of the way. You’ll receive real-time updates, accurate ETAs, and alerts for any delays or route changes, making bus management easy and secure.
View up-to-the-minute updates on vehicle locations, driver details, ETA, and other important bus information at any time.
Get immediate alerts in case of emergencies (SoS) or route deviations to ensure the safety of all passengers.
Track the bus in real-time, so you always know when it will arrive and if there are any unexpected delays.
Receive timely notifications when the bus enters or leaves pre-defined geofences, such as campus or office premises, keeping you informed of its movement.
